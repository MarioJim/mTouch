Why choose Knocki?

There is a wide variety of devices that are able to accomplish smart tasks at home, such as the Amazon Alexa, Samsung's SmartThings, Apple's HomePod, among others.
But, why should you choose Knocki? Knocki is simple, yet powerful.

Our competitors

Amazon EchoDot

Echo Dot is an intelligent, voice-controlled speaker. It connects via Wi-Fi to Alexa, a cloud-based voice service. Alexa can play music, answer questions, narrate news, check the weather forecast, set alarms, control compatible Smart House devices and much more.

How is Knocki better?
While Alexa may not hear you, Knocki is present in all of your surfaces. A simple gesture and the action you require is performed.

Apple HomePod

The HomePod is an intelligent loudspeaker that is a complement to Apple Music. It has excellent bass and consistent sound quality when playing different music genres. The speaker is extremely easy to install and Siri, its digital assistant, can hear you from the other corner of a room.

How is Knocki better?
The HomePod is only compatible with iOS devices. Siri and HomeKit lack the sophistication and compatibility of other devices, like Knocki.

Samsung SmartThings

The main goal of SmartThings is to provide a new class of automation by connecting appliances to one another, to the Internet, and to homeowners.

How is Knocki better?
Knocki can control everything SmartThings can, and also has many other integrations, as Nest devices and technologies like IFTTT.
