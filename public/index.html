Meet Knocki

A small wireless device that instantly transforms ordinary surfaces into powerful remote controls for the smart technologies around you.

What can it do?

Set or snooze an alarm.

Brew coffee from your bed.

Know if a door is knocked.

Turn your lights on/off.

Tap into your favorite tunes.

Find your lost phone.

Alert you when mail arrives.

Climate control with a tap.

And many more integrations.
